A CRM is only as good as the data flowing into it. The interesting work is rarely the UI —
it's the plumbing.

## Where revenue leaks

Most teams lose money in the gaps between systems: an ad platform that never tells the CRM
which lead converted, or a checkout that forgets the campaign that brought the customer in.

## Closing the loop

The fix is unglamorous: stable IDs, idempotent syncs, and a single source of truth for "what
is a customer." Once attribution is trustworthy, every downstream decision gets sharper —
budgets, segments, follow-ups.

Get the plumbing right and the dashboards take care of themselves.
